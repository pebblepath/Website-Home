Growing With You

The longer you use it,
the smarter it gets.

Most apps give you a static checklist. PebblePath builds a living model of your child, connecting milestones across time and domains to surface insights no generic tool can.

  • Pebble Daily — Parenting insights every morning.
  • Pebble Advisor — Family context by learning through milestones.
  • Weekend Planner — Location-based suggestions for your city.
  • Built for busy parents — real-time sync for multiple children.
  • Pediatrician-ready PDF exports — one tap, ready for every visit.
Privacy & Safety

Built with your child's
privacy at the center.

PebblePath was built parent-first. Every milestone, photo, and conversation about your child is treated as something we're holding for you, not collecting from you.

Family only

Your child's milestones, photos, and notes are visible only to the family members you invite. No public profiles, no social feed.

Private AI memory

PebblePath remembers your child so every answer gets more personal over time. The AI provider behind Pebble (Anthropic) doesn't.

Never sold, no ads.

We don't sell your data. We don't show ads. The only way we make money is the optional Premium subscription.

Yours to delete, anytime

One tap in Settings deletes your account, your children's profiles, your photos, and your history. Permanently.
