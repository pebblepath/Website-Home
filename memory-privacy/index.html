How we protect it

Family memory is sensitive. We've built security in layers — from how data moves between your phone and our servers, to what AI models ever see about your children.

Device-verified requests

Hardware attestation

On iOS, every request is signed by Apple's App Attest — a hardware-level proof that the request genuinely came from the PebblePath app on a real, unmodified device. No third-party script or automated scraper can call our servers.

Key never on device

AI calls go through our servers

The Anthropic API key that powers Pebble is not stored in the app. Every AI request routes through a Cloud Function — your phone never holds the key, so it can't be extracted from an install.

Names never exposed

PII tokenisation

Before your children's names reach an AI model, they are replaced with anonymous tokens like {{CHILD_NAME}}. The model never sees real names — they're restored only after the response comes back, inside our own servers.

Private messages stay private

Server-enforced private mode

When you send a private message in Pebble, a visibility rule on our Firestore database prevents the other parent from reading it — even on a modified client.

Optional on iOS

Biometric lock

You can enable Face ID or Touch ID to gate the app on open — so a borrowed phone can't surface your family's data. Default off; one tap to turn on in Settings → Security.

Website protection

Security headers on pebblepath.ai

Every page you load from pebblepath.ai carries Content Security Policy, HTTP Strict Transport Security, and Permissions Policy headers — enforced at the Cloudflare edge before the page reaches your browser.

Our commitments to your family

  • Your family data is never sold, and we run no advertising.
  • You can delete your account and all data at any time, from within the app.
  • Only the people you invite can see your family's data.
  • AI models see anonymised context — never raw names or identifiers.
  • What Pebble knows is visible and editable — in Settings → What Pebble knows.
  • We collect only what's useful within the next two weeks. Nothing more.
