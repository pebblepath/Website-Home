Pebble Memory & Privacy

How Pebble learns your family.
And how we keep it safe.

Pebble remembers the details of family life so you don’t have to. Here’s how that memory works — and how we keep it private.

Pebble Memory Family Circle Data Security
Where we stand

Six promises to your family.

Before the how, here are the lines we hold — the things we will and won’t do with your family’s information.

Never sold. No ads. Ever. Your family is the customer — never the product.
Only people you invite can see it. Access is locked in the database itself, not just hidden in the app.
The AI never sees real names. Your children’s names are swapped for anonymous tags before anything is sent.
Everything Pebble knows is yours. See it, edit it, or clear it anytime — nothing is hidden from you.
Delete it all, anytime. One tap in the app removes your account and every record we hold.
We keep only what’s useful now. Day-to-day context expires on its own — we don’t hoard the past.
Why it matters

A family’s memory shouldn’t live in twelve places.

Notes apps, group texts, camera rolls, Google Calendar, the daycare app, the school portal — and whatever a parent is holding in their head at any given moment. Nothing talks to anything. Pebble’s job is to become the one place it all lives, and the intelligence that connects it: the contextual brain of the family.

Not AI parenting advice. AI operational relief.
Parents don’t want another expert. They want a family chief of staff — something that quietly carries the coordination load so fewer things fall through the cracks.
Fewer forgotten things Calmer evenings Less coordination overhead Fewer decisions

How we protect it

Built to protect your family.

Family memory is personal, so we guard it at every layer. Here’s what that means for you — in plain terms.

Live

Locked to your family

Only the people you invite can open your family’s information. That lock lives in the database itself — not just the app — so there’s no way around it from a phone or a browser.

Live

The AI never learns who you are

Your children’s names and details are swapped for anonymous tags before anything reaches the AI. It helps your family without ever knowing who they are.

Live

Private stays private

Anything you mark private in Pebble is readable only by you — even a co-parent in the same family can’t see it, and it never feeds the shared family memory.

Optional · iOS

Lock the app with Face ID

Switch on Face ID or Touch ID so a phone left on the table — or handed to a little one — never opens your family’s data. Off by default; one tap in Settings.

Behind all of this: encrypted connections, every request verified to come from the real app on a real device, and strict database rules confirmed by a full security audit.
