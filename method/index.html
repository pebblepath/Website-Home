What we believe

Four ideas behind every milestone

The Method is a lens on trusted science, we don’t invent milestones, we connect and frame them so they actually help.

Through-lines, not checklists

We connect milestones into one path. Shared attention today becomes first words, then conversation, then reading. Showing the path is our signature.

What they can do, not what’s missing

We notice and grow what your child can do and what’s emerging next. Never “are they behind.” Encouraging by design, with a gentle door open to your pediatrician.

The whole child, the whole family

Development isn’t four clinical boxes in a vacuum. It lives in the everyday routines and relationships your family already shares.

It adapts to your family

Two languages? We count both.

A bilingual toddler can look “behind” on an English-only count, and be exactly on track the moment you count every word they know. So that’s what we do.

We treat language-mixing, code-switching, and a quiet stretch as the normal parts of growing up in two languages that they are.
